Navigate to the sample site URL (e.g., https://www.samplesite.com).
Click on the 'Login' button located at the top right corner of the homepage.
Enter your username in the 'Username' field.
Enter your password in the 'Password' field.
Click on the 'Submit' button to log in.
If the credentials are correct, you will be redirected to your dashboard.
If the credentials are incorrect, an error message will be displayed
